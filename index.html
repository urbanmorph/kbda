#ProPlanetBusiness

The pandemic threw up multiple challenges to the bicycle industry. The retailers were worst affected by the lockdown. On one hand bicycle sales were soaring because of the advantages it offered to society during the COVID wave, on the other hand the stores had to close and not be able to service the need. This created the need for KBDA to resurface, go to the Government of Karnataka and ask to be considered a necessary service. KBDA is an association wich will have 500 bicycle dealers across the state of Karnataka as members very soon. It aims to future proof the state and take bicycling in the state to the next level.

#Vision5Crore
We have an ambitious vision to sell 5 crore bicycles in Karnataka by 2024. We will achieve this by
  • Increasing our membership
  • Enabling conducive environment for the business
  • Engaging with Government and Public to execute market enhancing programs
Bicycle Training
We are a partner to the Certified Bicycle Training Program of DULT
  • This program puts more confident cyclists on the road
  • This will also increase the number of people who will want to ride a bicycle
  • This will ensure more engangement and empathy among public for cycling
Engagement
We curate events to foster better collaborations between businesses, society and the government.
  • Participate in Cycle Day events
  • conduct cycling awareness workshops
  • Conduct tradeshows & exhibitions for products
