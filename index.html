The future of collaboration

The pandemic threw up multiple challenges to the bicycle industry. The retailers were worst affected by the lockdown. On one hand bicycle sales were soaring because of the advantages it offered to society during the COVID wave, on the other hand the stores had to close and not be able to service the need. This created the need for KBDA to resurface and go to the Government of Karnataka as ansk to be considered a necessary service. KBDA is an association of more than 21 bicycle dealers/retailers across the state of Karnataka. It aims to future proof the state and take bicycling in the state to the next level.
